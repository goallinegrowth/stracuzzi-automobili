About Giovanni Stracuzzi

The Art of the
Vintage Supercar

Giovanni Stracuzzi has spent his life in pursuit of automotive perfection. Serving Miami and Palm Beach's most discerning collectors, Stracuzzi Automobili has become the trusted name for vintage Italian supercar service, restoration, and consignment in South Florida.

From the Lamborghini Miura to the Ferrari F40 — from the thundering Countach to the sublime Diablo — every vehicle entrusted to our care is treated as the irreplaceable work of art it is. These machines are not merely transportation; they are living history.

Our work is defined by an obsessive commitment to authenticity, period-correct craftsmanship, and the kind of mechanical knowledge that can only be earned through decades of hands-on experience with the world's greatest automobiles.

What We Do

Our Expertise

01

Restoration

We return legendary machines to their original glory — and beyond. Every component is inspected, rebuilt, or replaced to the highest possible standard, using period-correct parts and techniques that honour the original engineering.

  • Full mechanical restoration
  • Period-correct engine rebuilds
  • Bodywork & concours-level paint
  • Interior re-trimming & upholstery
  • Electrical system overhaul
  • Suspension & brake restoration
02

Consignment

Sell your vintage supercar through our global network of serious collectors and enthusiasts. We manage everything — professional documentation, valuation, worldwide marketing, negotiation, and secure transfer — so you don't have to.

  • Global collector network access
  • Professional photography & documentation
  • Market valuation & strategic pricing
  • Negotiation & buyer qualification
  • Secure transaction handling
  • Export & logistics coordination
